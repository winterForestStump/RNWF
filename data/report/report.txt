Between March 1, 2025, and March 1, 2026, there was a significant increase in total volume, with a 14.04% growth in RUB and a 29.42% increase in USD. However, from February to March 2026, the total volume in RUB and USD experienced slight decreases of 0.66% and 2.64%, respectively.

The liquid volume also saw substantial growth over the year, rising by 18.01% in RUB and 33.92% in USD. Yet, it decreased by 5.24% in RUB and 7.13% in USD from February to March 2026.

The Liquid/Total ratio in USD increased by 3.48% year-over-year but decreased by 4.61% from February to March 2026.
