Between December 2024 and December 2025, the total volume in RUB increased by 1.24%, reaching 13,259,020.6 million. In USD terms, the total volume saw a significant rise of 39.43% during the same period. However, the liquid volume in RUB decreased by 28.96% year-over-year, and the USD liquid volume experienced a more modest decline of 2.16%. The ratio of liquid to total volume in USD decreased by 29.83% from December 2024 to December 2025.
